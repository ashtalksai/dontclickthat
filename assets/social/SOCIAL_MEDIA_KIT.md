## Content Calendar Suggestions

- **Monday:** Motivational (start the week protecting family)

- **Wednesday:** Feature highlight
- **Thursday:** Testimonial/success story

### Monthly Themes

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Facebook
- **Focus:** Adult children (35-65) protecting aging parents
- **Content:** Emotional, family-focused, longer captions
- **Best times:** 9am, 1pm, 7pm EST
- **Use:** Facebook groups (caregivers, senior care)

### LinkedIn
- **Focus:** Professional caregivers, senior living facilities, B2B
- **Content:** Statistics, industry insights, thought leadership
- **Best times:** 8am, 12pm, 5pm EST weekdays
- **Use:** Articles, company updates, employee advocacy

### Twitter/X
- **Focus:** Tech-savvy seniors, younger advocates, quick tips
- **Content:** Short, punchy, timely (respond to scam news)
- **Best times:** 9am, 12pm, 5pm EST
- **Use:** Threads for education, replies for engagement

### Instagram
- **Focus:** Younger caregivers (25-45), visual storytelling
- **Content:** Stories, reels, carousel posts (educational)
- **Best times:** 11am, 2pm, 7pm EST
- **Use:** IGTV for longer explainers, Stories for daily tips
